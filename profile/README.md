# 🧠 CUGCG Research Group

Welcome to the **CUGCG Research Group**.

This organization hosts research projects and open-source code from the research group led by **Dr. Zheng Liu** at the School of Computer Science,  
China University of Geosciences (Wuhan).

Our group focuses on research in **3D vision, computer graphics, and deep learning for geometric data**, aiming to advance the understanding and processing of complex 3D data and real-world environments.

---

## 🔬 Research Areas

Our research interests include:

- 🧠 3D Deep Learning  
- 📐 Geometry Processing  
- 🎨 Computer Graphics  
- 👁️ 3D Vision  
- ☁️ Point Cloud Processing  
- 🌍 Digital Twin and Real-World 3D Modeling  

Applications include:

- 🚗 Autonomous Driving  
- 🏭 Smart Manufacturing  
- 🌐 Digital Twins  
- 🏗️ Real-World 3D Reconstruction  
- 🤖 Intelligent Perception Systems
